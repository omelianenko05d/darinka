about us

Why this museum exists

Vanished Things is a museum of forgotten objects, lost memories, and pieces of the past that slowly disappeared from everyday life.

Here you can explore old technology, nostalgic spaces, and silent traces of things that once felt ordinary, but now feel distant.

Our goal is to preserve not only objects, but also the emotions connected to them.
